Here's a timeline of Muhammad Ali's early life:

  • Jan 17 1942-Cassius Marcellus Clay, Jr is Born.

  • 1954-Muhammad is pointed to boxing by police officer to channel his anger

  • 1960-Muhammad Ali wins light-heavyweight gold medal at Summer Olympics

  • October 29 1960-Muhammad Ali Wins First Pro Fight

  • 1964-Cassius Clay changes his name to Muhammad Ali

  • Feb 25 1964-Ali defeats Sonny Liston for Heavyweight Title

  • 1967-Ali refuses induction into the US Army
